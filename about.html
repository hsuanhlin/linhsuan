Bio
A spatial and visual designer based in New York City, she specializes in conceptual research-based identity design, eye-catching installations and keen fashion aesthetic. She enjoys making meticulous investigations for well-informed visual solutions. Her versatility — acquired through learning across different disciplines — allows her to undertake every project with a modest and holistic perspective. Being a person in both the design field and the fashion industry makes her have a keen eye on aesthetics and at ease in a fast-paced environment.


Recognition
She earned a Pratt full scholarship 2019–2020 sponsored by Taiwan Ministry of Education. She also cooperated with both Clinique Shanghai and artists and filmmakers Lernert & Sander as a fashion model in TVC broadcasting in Asia Pacific region in 2019.
