Alarm for Laugh
Alarm

The theme of the project is ‘digital trust and laughter.’ Based on on-site research at Industry City, Brooklyn, it is of necessity for adult people to laugh as a daily routine in a group. To build trust and create laughter, there are four features in this alarm clock: a) laugh with strangers, b) draw with strangers, c) say thank you, and d) look at funny pieces.
