A NYC-based artist, designer and graduate student, Amber works in digital, print and experiential media from object to complex system. She is now pursuing her MFA at Pratt Institute and received a BFA from National Taiwan University of Arts in 2019. She is interested in value systems, and specialize in art direction and strategic thinking and dabble in experimentation with multidisciplinary arts, creative coding, and physical computing.
