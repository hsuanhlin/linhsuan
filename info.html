Amber Hsuan Lin

Info              Overview



A NYC-based, 23-year-old designer and multidisciplinary artist, Amber is now pursuing an MFA in Communications Design at Pratt Institute. She specializes in creative strategy, research-based identity design and experiential design, and dabbles in creative coding (this website is coded by herself). She enjoys making meticulous investigations for well-informed visual solutions. Her versatility — acquired through learning across different disciplines — allows her to undertake every project with a modest and holistic perspective.

Amber earned a Pratt Institute full scholarship 2019–2020 sponsored by Taiwan Ministry of Education. She also cooperated with both Clinique Shanghai and artists and filmmakers Lernert & Sander as a fashion model in TVC broadcasting in Asia Pacific region in 2019.

Amber loves questions, conversations, and collaborations. Please see full résumé, view design writing blog, connect via linkedin, or contact hlin27@pratt.edu for inquiries.
