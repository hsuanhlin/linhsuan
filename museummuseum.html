Museum Museum
Museum Identities in Museum

Museums display artworks and design works. What about museum display museums? This is a virtual exhibition on 25 Museums' identities regarding flow of architecture / environment and visual identity across Europe and United States. I intend to group museums through the scale of each museum’s physical covered-area on 'floorplan,' from the smallest to the largest, the lowest to the highest, so that visitors can walk through exhibition by immersive experience. As for an addition event for Museum Museum, I plan to conduct a hands-on workshop for children and teenagers. Ask them to bring 10-15 artworks, art crafts, daily-used objects, written notes, or music pieces to the workshop. And then, invite participants to ‘design’ an identity for their selected-pieces with a brief statement. The form of identity is not restricted and can be created utilizing self creativity.
