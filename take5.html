Take 5
Collaboration in Sound

Take 5 is an audio recorder toolkit that helps kids to explore the process of producing music collaborately - record, rearrange, edit, and mix the sounds immediately by hand. The toolkit includes 5 recorders and 1 sound-mixing plate. Once a recorder is placed on the plate - the active area, the plate starts to play the recorderâ€™s recordings. Now if someone else adds another recorder to the plate, they influences the sound. Kids can create different sound-mixing project by rearranging and sliding the positions of the recorders.
