I believe all built-in systems established in architecture and dance industries have variations, as well as similarities. In order to defamiliarize and illuminate the nature of a system, this research takes mundane as a new way of perspective to trigger and expand possibilities. Mundane is perceived and recognized differently by everyone culturally, politically, and scientifically. Mundane allows individuals to reinterpret and re-materialize in another field that further forms a new structure.

I aim to investigate sociology in multiplicity, and further repurpose the interactive functionality between objects and humans in physical spaces through visual activism.
